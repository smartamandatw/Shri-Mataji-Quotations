錫呂‧瑪塔吉‧涅瑪拉‧德維　靈性語錄

覺醒的跑道 It Depends on What Sort of Runway You Have

這取決於你有什麼樣的跑道。我沒辦法直接命令你把注意力放到內在，某種「發生」必須在你裡面真實地發生。

It depends on what sort of runway you have. I cannot just order you that you put your attention inside, some happening has to take place within you.

就像突然有東西掉落，你的注意力自然就會被吸引過去；同樣地，當昆達里尼覺醒的「發生」出現時，你的注意力也會被吸引到那個正在覺醒的地方——你開始看見覺醒，或者說，感覺到覺醒。但這一切發生得非常快，只在一瞬之間，快到有時候你什麼都沒察覺，只感覺到一股清涼的微風開始在你身體裡流動。

Supposing something suddenly falls off, then your attention goes there. In the same way, when the happening of the Kundalini awakening takes place, then your attention goes through that which is awakened there, and you just start seeing the awakening, or you can say, feel the awakening. But it is such a quick thing, a split of a second, that sometimes you miss everything except that you see the cool breeze has started flowing through you.

有時候你會清楚地感覺到：「就是這裡，就是這裡」——很多人都有這種感覺，覺得它終於升起了，就在這裡，還在跳動；然後你會知道，它已經開始運作了。這一切，取決於你有什麼樣的跑道——如果跑道崎嶇不平，你就會一路感覺到顛簸與起伏。

Sometimes you do feel, it's here, it's here, so many people feel that way also, it's come up now, and it's here throbbing, now it's alright, it has started working. It depends on what sort of runway you have — if the runway is horrid, then you go on feeling all the bumps and the dumps and everything.

有時候跑道毀損得太嚴重，昆達里尼可能需要花上好幾個月才能升起。不過我想，如今在霎哈嘉瑜伽裡，我們已經發展出這麼多方法與技巧，透過各種排列組合，現在要讓昆達里尼升起，已經容易得多了。

Sometimes the runways are so badly spoiled that it takes sometimes months for the Kundalini to rise, but now I think in Sahaja Yoga we have developed such methods and techniques, of such permutations and combinations, that nowadays it's much easier to raise the Kundalini.

1981年4月6日‧澳洲 Australia 霎哈嘉瑜伽在全世界各國推廣皆為公益免費
