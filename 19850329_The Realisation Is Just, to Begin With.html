錫呂‧瑪塔吉‧涅瑪拉‧德維　靈性語錄

覺醒只是開始 The Realisation Is Just, to Begin With

覺醒，只是一個開始。覺醒還不夠，它並不是救贖。覺醒，只是個起點。真正的救贖，是當我們掌握了自己的身體、掌握了自己的心智、掌握了自己——讓自己成為一朵美麗的花朵。而我們美麗的芬芳，將會流動出去，讓每個人都能享受。

The realisation is just, to begin with. Realisation is not sufficient. It's not the salvation. The realisation is just, to begin with. Salvation comes when we master our body, master our mind, master ourselves in such a way that we become beautiful flowers. And the fragrance of our beauty flows and everybody enjoys.

我說這些話，是對那些仍然認為——自己已經獲得覺醒、在霎哈嘉瑜伽裡有某種地位，所以就可以隨心所欲地對待他人的人說的。請記得，你為霎哈嘉瑜伽做了什麼，並不是重點；更重要的是，我對此有什麼樣的感受。

I'm speaking this for all the people who are still thinking that they have got realisation, they have certain positions in Sahaja Yoga, so they can misbehave with everyone as they like. Please remember that it is not important what you do for Sahaja Yoga, more important is what I feel about it.

如果你想要成為更偉大的人，就先讓自己謙卑下來。謙卑，是非常非常重要的。沒有人應該認為，我們可以透過支配他人，來達到更崇高的境界。

If you have to be something greater humble down yourself. Humbling down is very very important. Nobody has to think that we can achieve something greater heights by dominating others.
