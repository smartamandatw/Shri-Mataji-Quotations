錫呂‧瑪塔吉‧涅瑪拉‧德維　靈性語錄

純潔的欲望 Shuddha-Iccha

你必須深入去看。我已經告訴過你們了，那就是 Shuddha-Iccha，也就是昆達里尼，純潔的欲望——Shuddha-Iccha，純粹的欲望。因為其他的欲望永遠不會被滿足。你試著買一件東西，買了之後卻不覺得享受；於是你想再買另一件——還是不覺得享受；你又想買另一件——依然不覺得享受。這是因為它並不是一個「真實」的欲望。這就是經濟學的原理：你永遠不會滿足。你不斷地買、買、買、買、買、買，買了那麼多的東西……

You have to go deep down. I have already told you it is Shuddha-Iccha, is the Kundalini, Shuddha-Iccha – pure desire. Because other desires are never fulfilled. You try to buy one thing, then you don't enjoy it; you want to buy another one – you don't enjoy it; you want to buy another one – you don't enjoy it. So because it's not a 'real' desire. This is the principle of economics, that you are never satisfied. You go on buying, buying, buying, buying, buying, buying, and you buy so many things. ...

它有一種特質，就是無論你做什麼，都會反彈回到你自己身上。唯有靈性上的成長不會如此。它會綻放，它芬芳，它美麗。與自己同在，是如此美好的一種體驗，彷彿你走進了一座美麗的天堂花園。

It has a quality that whatever you do, repels (rebounds) back on you. Only the spiritual growth does not. It flowers, it is fragrant, it is beautiful. Such a beautiful experience to be with your self, as if you have entered into the beautiful garden of paradise.

如果你想擁有那樣的感受，就把所有無意義的念頭放下，把所有世俗的荒謬放下——把一切都放下，然後你會感覺到，你終於來到了你原本就該在的地方。你將不再有懷疑，不再有渴求，你也完全不會再感到任何一種不滿足。

If, that feeling, if you want to have, give up all non-sensical ideas, give up all worldly nonsense, everything you give up – and then you will feel that you are now very much there, where you should have been. You will have no doubts, you will have no hankerings, you will not feel at all in any way dissatisfied.
