淨化的力量 A Cleansing Force

事實上，當昆達里尼升起時，那是一種如此淨化的力量，祂會擊敗你所有的敵人——貪婪、憤怒、嫉妒——一切都會終結。於是你成為純淨的人格。

Actually, when Kundalini rises, it's such a cleansing force that She defeats all your enemies like greed, anger, jealousy – everything is finished. And you become pure personalities.

這是因為人類仍處於無知之中——但一旦他們知道自己真正是什麼，他們會驚訝於自己：原來他們是純淨的靈！那時你會明白，你不是這個世界，不是這個心智，不是這些情緒與才智，而你，是純淨的靈。

It is because human beings are ignorant – but once they know what they are, they'll be surprised at themselves: they are pure Spirit! Then you will realise you are not this world, this mind, these emotions, intelligence, but you are the pure Spirit.

這樣的純淨，正是這個世界今日極為需要的。如你所見，如今這個世界充滿動盪，各種衝突不斷發生——打著宗教的名義，或也許是神的名義，又或是某片土地的名義。

This purity is very much needed today in this world. Today, as you see, the world is full of turmoil, and all kinds of conflicts are going on. In the name of religion, or maybe in the name of God, in the name of some sort of a land.
