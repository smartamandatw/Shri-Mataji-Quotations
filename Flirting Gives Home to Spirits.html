眉來眼去，靈體棲身 Flirting Gives Home to Spirits

❖

如果你眉來眼去，你就是在為靈體（脫離肉身、仍遊蕩於世間的意識或能量體）提供棲身之所。

If you flirt, you are giving home to spirits.

喜歡用眼神調情的人，會招來另一種靈體寄居在自己身上——那些靈體在上一世是性無能、或對自己的性生活感到不滿足的人。這類靈體總是透過有著相同弱點的人來顯現自己。當中最惡劣的一種，會透過額輪運作，利用宿主的雙眼盯視他人，藉此進入另一個人的身體。

People who enjoy flirting with their eyes play host to other kinds of spirits who, in their last life, were impotent or dissatisfied with their sex life. Such spirits always manifest through people with the same weaknesses. The worst among them act through the Agnya Chakra, using their host's eyes to enter another person by staring at them.

現代社會並不把調情視為一種惡習，但事實上，沒有什麼比這個習慣更具傷害性了。一個沉溺於此的人，會對其他一切形式的享受失去興趣。享受調情所帶來的那種虛假快感，正是某些極為墮落的靈體在地球上顯現的一種精巧手法。透過它，他們摧毀了人類原本昇華過的愉悅感。這不僅對調情的人本身有害，它同時也成為一條管道，讓大量心懷不滿的靈魂，透過它源源不絕地湧入人類社會。

Modern society does not regard flirting as a vice, but nothing is more grievously harmful than this habit. A person who indulges in it loses interest in all other forms of enjoyment. The deceptive pleasure of enjoying flirting is the sophisticated way certain very depraved spirits manifest on Earth. By it, they obliterate a human's sublimated sense of pleasure. Not only is it harmful to the flirting individual, but it acts as a conduit for a stream of many dissatisfied souls to enter human society.

這些靈魂會以驚人的速度，從集體潛意識中傾瀉而出，透過一個發展出能夠自發控制其催眠般凝視或盯視能力的調情者進入人群。他把目光從一個人移到另一個人身上，以吸引異性之名，把靈體分派到他們身上。所有被吸引的人，都會接著做出同樣的舉動，並在自己毫不知情的情況下，淪為這些靈體的奴隸。

These souls pour in from the collective subconscious at a frightening speed through a flirt who develops the ability to spontaneously control his hypnotising gaze or stare. He shifts his eyes from one person to another, distributing spirits into them in the name of enticing the opposite sex. All those enticed take up the same play, and become enslaved by these spirits without knowing it.

有一位年輕女士曾向我坦承，她是從一位五十歲的老先生身上學會這個習慣的，如今這個習慣已經支配了她的整個人生。許多年輕人問我，該如何擺脫這種他們自己也感覺像病毒一樣不斷擴散、既骯髒又毫無意義的習慣。他們發現，自己因為這種奴役狀態而流失了大量的能量，而這也讓他們無法享受真誠性感受所帶來的真正喜悅。

A young lady once admitted to me that she picked up this habit from an old man of fifty, and it now dominated her life. Many young people ask me how to get rid of this filthy and pointless habit which they feel they are spreading like a virus. They find they lose a lot of energy through this enslavement, and it prevents them from enjoying the real joy of honest sexual feelings.

這種病態無論散播到哪裡，那裡的社會就會變得非常虛假、不自然。極早發生的性無能，就是這些習慣的實踐者所得到的「回報」，並由此開啟一個惡性循環：女性想要展露自己的身體，調情的欲望也隨之增加。而且，隨著性無能的加劇，人們會愈來愈依賴調情來獲得性方面的滿足。

Wherever this disease spreads, societies develop which become very artificial. Very early impotency is the reward for its practitioners, and it starts a vicious circle; women want to expose their bodies, and the desire to flirt increases. Moreover with impotency, more and more dependence is put on flirting for sexual satisfaction.

我認識一位富有的女士，她一心渴望自己看起來像個男人，並且想要與一位女性結婚。事實上，她被一個男性靈體附身了；當她擺脫那個靈體之後，她又重新變回了一個正常的女性。有些靈體只會短暫地進駐，卻能把它們的宿主塑造成一個極為喜怒無常的人。

A wealthy lady I know yearned to look like a man, and wanted to be married to a woman. In fact she was possessed by a male spirit, and when she got rid of the spirit she became a normal woman again. Some spirits enter for a short time and make a very temperamental personality out of their host.
