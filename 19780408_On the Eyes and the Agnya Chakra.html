清澈之眼 On the Eyes and the Agnya Chakra

❖

眼睛非常重要。你看，它們就在額輪上，它們是窗戶。你看，從眼睛，應該只有「出去」，沒有「進來」。應該是這樣的——一雙如此清澈的眼睛，讓一切只從眼睛流向他人。它必須絕對地清澈，不接收任何東西進來。這樣才是對的，你才可以去看著人們。

Eyes are very important. You see, they are on the Agnya Chakra. They are the windows. See, from the eyes it should be only going, nothing coming. It should be so, such a clear eye that from the eyes it's only going to others. Absolutely, it should be clear. Nothing taking in. Then it's all right, you can look at people.

連我自己都很少直視人們，因為我有時候會擔心，他們可能會因此暈厥，或是突然被昆達里尼衝擊。這也是為什麼我會有點擔心這種事發生在我身上——因為，即使是這麼多的愛，也只有極少數人能夠承受得住。

Even I don't look at people because sometimes I think that they might faint, or they might get a Kundalini suddenly up and all that. That's why I'm a little bit worried about that might happen with Me, because so much love also very few people can take in, you see.

眼睛應該絕對地清澈，只朝外流動，不從他人那裡接收任何東西進入眼睛——因為你所接收到的，其實是污穢與骯髒。人們就是這樣，透過眼睛，接收了各種靈體，而這些靈體，就這樣進入了你自己。大多數這類導師，會觸碰你的額輪，透過這個方式，把靈體放進去。

Eyes should be absolutely clear, going outward, not receiving anything into the eyes from others because what you receive is dirt and filth. This is how you receive all the spirits from the eyes, and they go into yourself. Most of these gurus touch your Agnya and put the spirits into it through this.
