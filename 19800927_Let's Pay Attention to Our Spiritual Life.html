錫呂‧瑪塔吉‧涅瑪拉‧德維　靈性語錄

靈性優先 Let's Pay Attention to Our Spiritual Life

我們把太多的注意力放在其他事情上，卻很少放在自己的靈性生命上。為什麼會這樣呢？為什麼我們更在乎物質的東西、物質的享受、物質的成就、物質的執念？

We pay so much attention to other things than to our spiritual life. Why is it? Why is it we pay more attention to material things, to material comforts, to material achievements, to material obsessions?

當你開始使用物質，漸漸地，你會變得懶散，成為物質的奴隸——它會形成一種習慣。如果你習慣坐椅子，你就無法坐在地上；如果你習慣了舒適的生活，你就再也過不了不舒適的生活。物質，就是這樣在不知不覺間奴役著你。

I mean to say that when you start using the matter gradually you become lethargic. You become slave to it, it forms a habit. If you use a chair then you cannot sit on the ground. If you are used to a comfortable life, you cannot live in an uncomfortable life. Sort of it slaves you.

覺醒之後，如果你仍然認同於物質，你的覺醒就會變得遲緩。所以我們必須完全理解——物質，終究是次要的。那麼，什麼是物質呢？物質，是由五大元素組成的，正是這五大元素，構成了我們的身體；它們在人體的右側運作，會把你推向左脈，或拉向右脈。

After realization if you are still identified with the matter, then your realization slows down. So one has to understand fully that matter is absolutely secondary. Now what is matter? Matter, you know, is made out of five elements, which have made this body of ours. And these five elements are on the right-hand side of the human beings, and either push you towards the left side or pull you towards the right side.

另一方面，如果你試圖去駕馭物質、對物質過度用心——把每件事都做得盡善盡美，對一切都追求高效率——你的自我就會膨脹。而當自我膨脹時，你便走向了暴力。

Secondly, if you try to overpower the matter, getting overactive with matter, you make everything properly and nicely, you are over efficient with the matter, then your ego develops and when your ego develops you go into violence.
