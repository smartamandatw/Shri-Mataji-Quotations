錫呂‧瑪塔吉‧涅瑪拉‧德維　靈性語錄

頭腦受靈所啟迪 The Spirit Enlightens the Brain

這是中脈之路，是瑪哈拉克希米之路，是引領你進入宇宙大我狀態、進入頭腦狀態的道路。此時，三股力量開始結合運作：頭腦是宇宙大我，心是濕婆，肝臟是梵天——這三種力量，都在其中運作著。

This is the Sushumna path. It is the Mahalakshmi's path. It is the path, which leads you to the state of Virat, to the state of your brain. So now, the combination of three things work out. The brain is the Virat, heart is Shiva, and the liver is Brahmadeva. All these three powers are there.

但在你獲得覺醒的那一刻，你的頭腦便臣服於濕婆，臣服於濕婆的原理，也就是靈。這種臣服，並非出於強迫，而是為了吸收靈的力量——頭腦完全臣服於靈，於是靈便照亮了頭腦。

But at that time, when you are realized, then your brain surrenders to Shiva, to the principle of Shiva, that is the Spirit. Brain is surrendered, not by force or anything, but to absorb the power of the Spirit, the brain gets surrendered completely to the Spirit, so that the Spirit enlightens the brain.

正是頭腦被照亮之後，才讓你擁有這種精微的理解。它讓你的頭腦開始明白——原來有這麼多喜悅的發生，人們一次又一次地被祝福著；於是他們感到驚訝，頭腦開始思考：「我怎麼會得到這樣的祝福？這是怎麼發生在我身上的？我是如何獲得這樣的轉化的？」於是，他們漸漸開始信任自己的心，也就是開始信任自己的靈，開始信任自己的虔誠。也正是如此，虔誠奉獻才能在毫無欲求、毫無索求、毫無義務的狀態下完成，純粹只是一種合一——可以說，這是因為頭腦與心，終於攜手合一了。

It is the enlightenment of the brain that gives you all this subtle understanding. There are so many blissful happenings, people are blessed: once, twice, thrice – then they are surprised, the brain starts thinking, "How did I get this blessing, how did it happen to me, how could I get my transformation?" So slowly-slowly they start trusting their heart, they start trusting their Spirit, they start trusting their devotion. And this is how the bhakti is absolutely done without any desire, without any asking, without any obligation, just in oneness — because the brain joins hands with the heart.

此時，梵天也同樣臣服了。結果是——你的肝臟臣服於你的頭腦。當這一切發生，無論你做什麼——無論是唱歌，還是身為一名公務員，或是任何其他事情——只要是透過頭腦、透過生殖輪，或透過梵天所完成的，都成了被照亮的行動。

Now, also the Brahmadeva becomes surrendered. As a result, your liver is surrendered to your brain. When this happens, whatever you do — whether you sing, whether you're a government servant, whether anything — whatever you do through the Swadishthana or through the Brahmadeva, it is an enlightened work.

因為被照亮的，是與這無所不在的力量相連結的頭腦，所以無論你在做什麼，無論你有什麼樣的想法，完全的照亮，意味著你終將達成它們。被照亮的頭腦，無論它想著什麼，那既是思考，同時也是成就——兩者同時發生。要明白的是，一個被照亮的人，擁有成就任何事的力量；但與此同時，他的欲望，也將會是神聖的。

As it is the brain which is enlightened, connected with this All Pervading Power, whatever you are doing, whatever your ideas are, being absolutely enlightened means that you achieve them. Enlightened brain, whatever it thinks, it is thinking as well as achieving – both together. An enlightened person has the power to achieve anything. But at the same time his desires would be Divine.

備註：中脈（Sushumna）是連結頭腦與靈的通道；瑪哈拉克希米、濕婆、梵天分別是霎哈嘉瑜伽中代表淨化、純淨意識與行動創造的三種原理；生殖輪（Swadishthana）則與創造力、思考力有關。
